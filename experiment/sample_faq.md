# Support & Billing FAQ

## Account & Billing

Your subscription renews automatically each month on the day you first signed up. You can see your next billing date at any time under Account Settings > Billing. We accept all major credit cards and, for annual plans, wire transfer on request.

Upgrading your plan takes effect immediately and we charge a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle. Downgrading takes effect at the start of your next billing cycle, so you keep access to your current plan's features until then.

## Refund Policy

We offer a full refund within 14 days of your initial purchase, no questions asked — just email support@example.com with your account email and order number. After the 14-day window, refunds are issued only for verified billing errors, such as being charged twice for the same period.

Annual plans cancelled after the 14-day window are not refunded for unused months, but you can cancel auto-renewal at any time to avoid future charges.

## Data Privacy

We never sell customer data to third parties. Data you upload is encrypted at rest and in transit, and is only accessed by automated systems unless you explicitly grant support access to debug an issue on your account.

You can request full deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from Account Settings > Privacy > Delete My Data. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days and cannot be undone.

## Support Channels

Free-tier customers get community forum support with a typical response time of 1-2 business days. Paid plans include email support with a same-business-day response target, and our top-tier Enterprise plan includes a dedicated Slack channel with a 4-hour response SLA during business hours.

For urgent production outages on paid plans, use the "Urgent" priority flag when filing a ticket — this pages the on-call support engineer regardless of plan tier.
